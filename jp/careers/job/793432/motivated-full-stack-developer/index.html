OUR VISION FOR THE FUTURE

Up until now all mobile subscriptions have been the same, but we are changing this. People with a subscription from a Working Group Two powered mobile operator will soon enjoy a much richer set of features, and will be able to personalize their subscription by enabling products from our product marketplace. We will make this happen by enabling third party developers to create products that people love, and by helping operators distribute these products to their subscribers.

Our goal is to create a product ecosystem, which consists of three main components:

  • A developer portal: Enabling developers to use APIs and media streams for Messaging, Data, and Voice, and to submit their product to our marketplace. 

  • An operator marketplace: Allowing mobile operators to buy, manage, and distribute products to their subscribers.

  • A subscriber marketplace: Letting mobile subscribers find, activate and launch the products they want to add to their subscription.
